# Strategy

## Vision
AI-powered trend curation platform. Automatically scans trending products from various brands and aggregates them on a single site targeting Gen Z. Revenue: affiliate commission + ads.

## Current Priorities
1. Get trend analysis system running (Fashion Trend Agent)
2. Build curation website (main platform)
3. Start content production (trend articles, product cards)
4. Set up Instagram as a traffic channel

- Dropshipping
- Own manufacturing
- Luxury segment
- Mobile app (at launch)

## Business Model
- **Revenue 1 — Affiliate:** User clicks a product on the site → goes to the brand → if they buy, we earn commission (typically 5–15%)
- **Revenue 2 — Ads:** As traffic grows, ad revenue (AdSense, sponsored content)
- **Revenue 3 — Long term:** As audience grows, brand partnerships and sponsorships
- **Cost:** Domain (~$10/year), hosting (~$5–20/month), ad budget (optional)
- **Risk:** Very low — no inventory, no product, just content and traffic
