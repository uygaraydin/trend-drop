# Audience

## Who They Are
- **Generation:** Gen Z (born 1997–2012, currently ages 14–29)
- **Location:** Turkey (primary), global (secondary)
- **Income:** Low-to-mid (students, recent grads, first job)
- **Digital behavior:** Mobile-first, lives on Instagram and TikTok, watches YouTube Shorts
- **Shopping habits:** Online-first, impulsive, decides based on social proof (reviews, influencers)
- **Fashion approach:** Trend-driven but budget-conscious, fast fashion consumer, open to thrift/vintage

## Pain Points
- "Good clothes are expensive" — wants a luxury look but lacks the budget
- "I don't know what to wear" — wants to follow trends but has no time
- "Everyone wears the same thing" — wants to look different but doesn't know how
- "Nothing I buy online looks like the photo" — trust issues

## Goals
- Look trendy and stylish
- Dress well without breaking the budget
- Get likes/validation on social media
- Find their own personal style

## Language
- Turkish + English mixed ("cok vibe", "bu outfit fire", "aesthetic")
- Heavy on emoji and slang
- Short, punchy sentences
- "Bence bu cok overrated", "drip", "fit check", "inspo"

## Segments

### Segment 1: Trend Follower (Primary segment)
- **Who:** 18–24, university student or recent grad
- **Pain:** Doesn't know what's trending, limited budget
- **Goal:** Look good on Instagram, don't miss trends
- **Budget:** 100–400 TL per piece
- **Shops at:** Zara, Bershka, Pull&Bear, Trendyol, Shein

### Segment 2: Streetwear Enthusiast
- **Who:** 16–22, skews male
- **Pain:** Hard to find unique pieces, expensive
- **Goal:** Unique fit, street style, sneaker culture
- **Budget:** 200–600 TL per piece (more for statement pieces)
- **Shops at:** Nike, Stussy, New Balance, secondhand platforms

### Segment 3: Minimal / Clean Girl
- **Who:** 18–26, skews female
- **Pain:** Hard to find quality basics
- **Goal:** Effortless chic, capsule wardrobe vibe
- **Budget:** 150–500 TL per piece
- **Shops at:** Zara, Mango, H&M Premium, COS
